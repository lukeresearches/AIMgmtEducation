Hypothesis Formation

Prioritization

Can the hypothesis be ranked against others for its importance to the theory and impact on decision-making

Coherent

How well does each hypothesis align with the overarching theory of value?

Simple

Could your hypotheses be simplified (so they only identify a single part, or a single connection)?

Falsifiable

Can you clearly describe what would prove each hypothesis wrong (so that you can determine if it's true or false)?
